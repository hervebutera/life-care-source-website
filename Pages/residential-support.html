Residential Support Services

Personalized Care in a Supportive Environment

At Life Care Community, we provide high-quality residential homes in welcoming neighborhoods for adults with developmental disabilities. Our philosophy centers around each individual’s unique needs, ensuring compassionate, supportive, and expert care. Our homes offer up to 24 hours of personal care, with residents choosing their level of care, living arrangements, and daily routines, all designed to enhance their quality of life.

Our Residential Homes

Life Care Community’s residential homes offer comprehensive, around-the-clock care for two to five adults in a homelike setting. We prioritize understanding each resident’s needs, preferences, and backgrounds to ensure a smooth and successful transition into their new home. Our approach is built on creating a safe, nurturing, and empowering environment where residents feel valued and supported.

Commitment to Quality Care

As our mission aligns with empowering individuals with developmental disabilities to live fulfilling lives, we focus on person-centered supports that enable each resident to experience their best life, fostering independence and personal growth.
